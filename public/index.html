How long does a typical project take?
Conquest (landing page) delivers in 14 days. Dominion (full website) in 28 days. Empire (platforms) timelines are scoped per project — typically 8–16 weeks. We don't rush craft, but we don't miss deadlines either.
Do you work with startups or only established businesses?
Both — with one condition. We work with founders who are serious about building something that lasts. Whether you're pre-revenue or post-Series-A, what matters is your commitment to quality and your long-term vision.
What does the onboarding process look like?
After a brief discovery call, we send a proposal within 48 hours. Once approved, you receive a structured onboarding questionnaire and project brief. Work begins within one week of contract signing.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
All project-based engagements include 30 days of post-launch support. After that, clients can transition to a monthly retainer (Vanguard, Ascent, or Overlord) for continuous optimization and support.
Why are your prices higher than most agencies?
Because we deliver outcomes, not outputs. Our clients don't pay for hours or deliverables — they pay for results that compound over years. The cost of cheap is always higher than the cost of quality. This is the only truth we know.
