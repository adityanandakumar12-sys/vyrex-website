Before You Ask

QUESTIONS
ANSWERED.

How fast do you deliver? +
Landing pages in 7 days. Full websites in 14–21 days. Apps in 4–8 weeks depending on scope. Every project gets a precise timeline on day one — no vague "it depends".
Do you work with startups? +
Yes. Some of our best work is with pre-revenue startups who need to look like a $10M company on a $2k budget. We know how to make that happen.
What's included in the free audit? +
A full teardown of your website, SEO, conversion funnel, and competitor positioning. You'll receive a detailed report within 48 hours — with specific actions, not vague advice.
Do you take equity? +
Rarely. On a case-by-case basis for exceptional early-stage companies. We prefer cash — it keeps our incentives aligned with your ROI, not your cap table.
How do payments work? +
50% upfront, 50% on delivery for one-time projects. Retainers are monthly, cancel anytime with 30-day notice. We accept USD via bank transfer, Stripe, or Wise.
Can you handle everything end-to-end? +
Yes. Strategy, design, development, copywriting, SEO, ads — the Siege retainer gives you a full embedded team. You focus on your product; we handle your growth.
What industries do you work in? +
SaaS, e-commerce, consulting, healthcare tech, fintech, fashion, and more. If you have a great product and need more customers, we can help.
Do you offer guarantees? +
We guarantee delivery on-spec, on-time. For retainer clients, if we don't hit agreed KPIs in 90 days, we work the next month free.
