Clarity Before
Commitment

No hidden fees. No scope creep. No vague timelines.

Every engagement begins with complete transparency — you know exactly what you are getting, when you are getting it, and what it costs.

What is your typical project timeline?
Landing pages: 7–10 business days. Full websites: 3–4 weeks. Apps and platforms: 6–12 weeks depending on scope. We never pad timelines. When we give a date, we meet it.
How does payment work?
50% upon kickoff, 50% upon delivery. Retainers are billed monthly in advance via Stripe. All payments in USD. We accept all major cards and wire transfer for larger engagements.
Do you offer revisions?
Every project includes three full rounds of revisions. In practice, most clients use one — because our initial work is rarely far from the target. We aim to be right the first time.
What industries do you serve?
Finance, SaaS, e-commerce, professional services, healthcare, and consumer brands. We are industry-agnostic in capability but selective in clients — we work with brands serious about excellence.
How do I get started?
Email hello@aevrix.io or use the contact section below. We schedule a 30-minute discovery call within 24 hours. No sales pressure — just an honest conversation about whether we are the right fit.
